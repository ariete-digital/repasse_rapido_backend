Primeira implantação:

1) Configurar o site pelo forge

2) Alterar variavel de ambiente
QUEUE_CONNECTION=database

3) Supervisord instalado na VPS da Digital Ocean, criar o arquivo:

/etc/supervisor/conf.d/laravel-worker.conf

[program:laravel-worker]
process_name=%(program_name)s_%(process_num)02d
command=php /home/forge/api.queroauto.com.br/artisan queue:work --sleep=3 --tries=3 --timeout=3600
autostart=true
autorestart=true
numprocs=1
redirect_stderr=true
stdout_logfile=/home/forge/api.queroauto.com.br/storage/logs/worker.log

4) Ativar o supervisor
sudo supervisorctl reread
sudo supervisorctl update
sudo supervisorctl start laravel-worker:*
